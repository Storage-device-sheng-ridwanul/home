HDD or SSD For Gaming?.

Why does it Matter?

Storage drives store games and any preloaded files for online gameplay. You will need enough storage space to store
your games and anything else that you may have on your computer. HDD (Hard drives) and SSD (Solid-state drives) are
options that a consumer has to decide on. They both store data but are both different from the perspective of the technology for
these storage devices.

HDD V.S. SDD

1. Access Time

Access time is total time it takes a computer to request data, and then that request to be met.
HDD access times are much slower than SSD because HDD have moving parts, like spinning plates and moving
read/write heads. To access needed data the heads must move to the tracks on the spinning plates where data
is located. And the SSD

2. Reliability

SSDs can survive in extreme and harsh environments because they don't have moving parts such as actuator
arms unlike it's mechanical competition. SSDs can withstand accidental drops and other shocks, vibration, extreme
temperatures, and magnetic fields better than HDDs.
